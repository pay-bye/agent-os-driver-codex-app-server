# Code of Conduct

Codex app-server driver project spaces are working spaces. Participants keep technical discussion
direct, specific, and respectful.

## Expected Conduct

- Use precise technical claims and provide evidence when disputing behavior.
- Keep feedback focused on the work, not the person.
- Respect maintainer decisions about scope, safety, and release readiness.
- Do not post secrets, credentials, private operational data, or customer data.

## Unacceptable Conduct

- Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private contact information or credentials.
- Disrupting issue, review, or release processes.
- Repeatedly reopening decisions without new technical evidence.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove comments, close issues, block accounts, or restrict participation
when conduct disrupts project work or safety. Security-sensitive conduct is handled through the
security reporting process.
